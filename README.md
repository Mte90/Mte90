[Daniele Scasciafratte](https://twitter.com/mte90net), the Open Source Multiversal guy (founder of [Italian Linux Society Rieti](https://rieti.ils.org/)), he should work in his web agency, [Codeat](https://github.com/CodeAtCode) in Rome, and in the rest of the day collaborates in many open source projects.  
All his talks and slide with recordings are [avalaible on his dedicated website](https://mte90.tech/).   
Since April 2021 part of the council of [Italian Linux Society](https://ils.org) association.
In 2024 he joined the [Amber-Lang](https://amber-lang.com/) project to create a Bash transpiler as one of the co maintainers.

Today experiencing in AI Spec Driven Development but with Old School vibes, [a multi LLM bug scanner orchestrator](https://github.com/CodeAtCode/baco-scanner), ["kate-agents" a clone for Kate editor of the Zed feature](https://github.com/Mte90/kate-agents), [AvoidRain a ThreeJS game](https://github.com/Mte90/avoidrain), [soundKonverter a port to modern Qt/KDE](https://github.com/Mte90/soundkonverter), [solopreneur a manual/skill](https://github.com/Mte90/solopreneur), [ToneCraft for Thunderbird](https://github.com/Mte90/ToneCraft) and [with his own Open Source skillset](https://github.com/CodeAtCode/oss-ai-skills).

A Browser Extension developer with [Share Backported](https://github.com/Mte90/Share-Backported), [Footswitch Scripted](https://github.com/Mte90/pydal) with [scripting addictions](https://github.com/Mte90/My-Scripts), [Enforcer of OSS license in projects](https://github.com/Mte90/GH-License), [Addicted typo generator/fixer](https://github.com/Mte90/espanso-typofixer) and also a Retrogaming tool developer for rom cheats with [GGGG](https://github.com/Mte90/Game-Genie-Good-Guy) and [GBAATM-Rebirth](https://github.com/Mte90/GBAATM-Rebirth).

## Past Open Source experiences
In the [WordPress world he was a Core Contributor](https://profiles.wordpress.org/mte90/), it was a plugin developer (and PTE because is the creator of [GlotDict](https://github.com/Mte90/GlotDict) that is maintaining) and [WordPress Plugin Boilerplate Powered](https://github.com/WPBP/) author and one of the ex-maintainers of [VVV](https://github.com/Varying-Vagrant-Vagrants).  
In the meantime also a long time experience volunteer in the [Mozilla community](https://mozillians.org/it/u/Mte90/) (part of [Mozilla Italia](https://github.com/MozillaItalia)), a former [Mozilla TechSpeakers](https://wiki.mozilla.org/TechSpeakers) and former [Mozilla Rep](https://wiki.mozilla.org/ReMo) (former Reps Council member) .  


He had an [🇮🇹 podcast](https://daniele.tech/podcast/) about Open Source and tech opinions every week (197 episodes!) for 4 years.
